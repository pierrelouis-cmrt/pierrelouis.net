The design of mechanical characters ↗
Paper from Disney
In 2013, Disney published a paper on the design of mechanical characters. It's basically an interactive design system that lets anyone (even without engineering experience) turn a digital, articulated character into a 3-D-printable mechanical toy/figure. It works for a wide range of characters and movements, from simple 2D to complex 3D animations.
